Intructions
  • Step 1. Preheat the oven to 350 F.
  • Step 2. Beat eggs in a small bowl
  • Step 3. Add cake mix and olive oil to the beaten eggs and mix well
  • Step 4. Beat in chocolate chips (batter will be very thick)
  • Step 5. Grease a 9 X 13 baking dish (see notes for thickness)
  • Step 6. Spread batter evenly in the dish (this will take several minutes to spread evenly)
  • Step 7. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 - 25 minutes, or until toothpick inserted comes out clean
  • Step 8. Once cooled, cover or store in an airtight container
  • Step 9. Enjoy!
Ingredients
  • 1 (15 oz) box of yellow cake mix
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1/3 cup olive oil (can substitute melted butter or vegetable oil also)
  • 12 oz bag of chocolate chips
Tools
  • 1 Medium Bowl
  • 1 Whisk
  • 1 Baking Dish
