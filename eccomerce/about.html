Who Are We?

hello, we are Cliffer! A rock climbing company that is dedicated to rock climbers and to mountaineers. Rock climbing experience is not required.

Climbing class includes pre- and post-training, as well as instructor-level mentoring.

Competitive climbing is an asset.

Basic knowledge of climbing equipment, belay, and safety skills are highly recommended.

Please Respect Nature: The climbing area is steep and it is not recommended for children under 12 years.

Does not include fall protection.

All climbers will be equipt with their own protection, belay device, and footwear.

Fun Fact: Rock climbing can burn between anywhere from 500 to as much as 900 calories per hour also a study found that rock climbing is as good for cardio as running 8-11 minutes per mile.
